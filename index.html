Questions

Fair questions, honest answers

Is Pocket Breath Coach really free?

Yes — the free version is a real app, not a teaser. You get seven complete guided sessions, fully adjustable breathing patterns, reminders, favorites and the sleep timer, with no time limits and no account required. Premium unlocks the full library of sessions, scenes and sounds.

How is it different from Calm or Headspace?

Calm and Headspace are broad meditation platforms built around courses and narrated content. Pocket Breath Coach does one thing: paced slow breathing, with precise control over your rhythm and visuals and sound that follow it. There's nothing to keep up with — and Premium costs $6.99 a year instead of $69.99.

Which breathing techniques are included?

Box breathing, 4-7-8, even 1:1 rhythms, calming 4:6 and 1:2 extended-exhale ratios, and pranayama-inspired patterns — over 260 preset variations. Every preset is adjustable, down to a tenth of a second per phase, and you can save your own.

Can it help me fall asleep?

Sleep is what the app is best at. Long-exhale sessions slow things down, night scenes keep the light low, and the sleep timer fades the sound out and ends the session after you've drifted off. Audio keeps playing with the screen locked.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Free sessions work entirely offline, and Premium lets you download the full sound library to your phone. Airplane mode is arguably the best way to use it.

What do you do with my data?

As little as possible. There are no ads, no third-party trackers and no analytics — we deliberately removed them. Your favorites and history stay on your device unless you create an account to sync them. See the privacy policy.
