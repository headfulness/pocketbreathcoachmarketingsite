Delete Your Account

How to delete your Pocket Breath Coach account and data

You can delete your Pocket Breath Coach account and all associated data at any time, directly from within the app. Follow the steps below.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. In the Pocket Breath Coach app, go to the Profile page.
  2. Tap the settings icon.
  3. Log in. If you are already logged in, it will say “Logged in as yourname@email.com”.
  4. Below that, tap the Delete Account button.
  5. A confirmation message will pop up. Confirm it, and your account and associated data will be deleted.

Please note: Deleting your account is permanent. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data are permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, your personal account information and the data associated with it are permanently removed from our systems. Certain anonymized usage data and log data that is not linked to your identity may be retained as described in our Privacy Policy.

Need Help?

If you have any trouble deleting your account, or have questions about your data, please contact us at luke@pocketbreathcoach.com and we will be happy to assist.
